Requirements of a conformant installation

In order to meet the EMC requirements, the WebBrick need to be correctly installed. Here are the specific installation recommendations:

DC Supply

 Each WebBrick needs 110mA for itself and can supply a further 100mA a 5V. It is acceptable to draw 500mA from each of the open collector outputs. Excess load on the 5V supply from the WebBrick will increase EM emmissions.

Cabling

 It is recommendend that CAT5 (CAT5E, CAT6, CAT7) cable is used to connect digital and analogue panels and equipment to the WebBrick. Core Pairs should have one side grounded to suppress emissions. The WebBrick has a suitable ground for this purpose.
